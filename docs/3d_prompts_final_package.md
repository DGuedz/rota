# ROTA: 3D Asset Prompts Final Package

Este pacote contém os 4 prompts finais otimizados para a geração ou integração de assets 3D em cada seção principal da Landing Page da ROTA. Eles seguem as diretrizes rígidas do "Institutional Brutalism" e garantem a infraestrutura visual sem comprometer performance ou legibilidade.

---

## 1. Hero Section (Global Agent Mesh)
**Formato Recomendado:** Scroll-driven video background.
**Prompt para o Antigravity / IA 3D:**
> "Integrate this 3D asset as the background of the ROTA website's Hero Section. The animation must visually represent a global mesh of autonomous economic nodes, economic routes, skill discovery, and on-chain settlement. Place the asset strictly as a background layer with a subtle institutional gradient overlay to ensure all text remains perfectly readable. The section must be pinned while the animation advances with the scroll; scrolling down progresses the video, scrolling up rewinds it. Upon completion, normal page scroll resumes. The motion must be soft, premium, and controlled—no cyberpunk exaggeration, no distracting particles. Keep it muted, without visible controls, and seamlessly integrated. *Crucial:* If the scroll-scrubbing stutters, re-encode the MP4 with FFmpeg forcing every frame to be a keyframe (`-g 1`) for perfectly smooth playback."

---

## 2. Product Pillars (Discover / Contract / Settle)
**Formato Recomendado:** Video cards (loop contínuo e mudo).
**Prompt para o Antigravity / IA 3D:**
> "Integrate these three 3D micro-assets into the 'Pillars' feature cards of the ROTA website. They represent 'Skill Discovery' (a structured, searchable network), 'Contract' (intent, escrow, and controlled release), and 'Settle' (atomic settlement across ledgers). Implement them as embedded video elements inside the cards with continuous, seamless, and muted loops. The aesthetic must remain elegant, minimal, and infrastructure-like (Institutional Brutalism)—avoid sci-fi tropes. Ensure the videos do not overpower the typography and maintain the high-credibility on-chain product feel."

---

## 3. Developer / GitHub Distribution
**Formato Recomendado:** Componente visual técnico (loop).
**Prompt para o Antigravity / IA 3D:**
> "Use this 3D asset to represent the technical developer experience and GitHub distribution of the ROTA protocol. It should look like a CLI rail or an execution pulse—a visual metaphor for programmable rails where agents route value. Integrate it as an embedded visual component next to the developer/documentation section. Keep the animation strictly technical, dry, and highly controlled. Muted, continuous loop, avoiding excessive motion that distracts from the code snippets and developer copy."

---

## 4. Metrics & Proof
**Formato Recomendado:** Fundo sutil animado.
**Prompt para o Antigravity / IA 3D:**
> "Integrate this 3D asset as a near-static animated background for the 'Metrics & Proof' section of the ROTA website. The visual should consist of discrete economic traffic lines, subtle pulses, or a faint ledger glow. It must act purely as a semantic texture that reinforces public proof and institutional volume without stealing attention from the large data numbers (e.g., '14,200+ Skills Indexed'). Maintain the highest level of text contrast. Loop continuously and seamlessly."
